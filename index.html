HOW PROVIDE WORKS

From ZIP code
to food on the table.

01 — Search

ZIP Discovery

Enter a ZIP code and we geocode your area, centering the map on your neighborhood so the search starts where you live.

02 — Explore

Live Resource Map

SNAP retailers, food pantries, free meal sites, and WIC locations appear on the map; filter by type and adjust radius to see what's nearby.

03 — Act

Resources at Hand

Compare distance, hours, and contact details for each location, then open directions or call directly from the dashboard.
